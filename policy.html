You can delete your account and all related-date stored on our server. From inside KIEMLUA app, go to Account tab > My Account and select "Delete account" button. Your request will be processed within 72-hours. Please note that we will wipe all data related to you so it's impossible to recovery.
If you do not come online at least once within 24 months, your account will be deleted along with all data with all transactions and other information. The changes will take effect on January 9th 2023.
